Chicken Salad

Ingredients

Making the perfect Chicken Salad has some technique to it.

But with some practice and help anyone can make it.

  • Chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chipotle Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken in smaller pieces
  2. Place the lettuce around the chicken
  3. Mix in some tomatoes to the lettuce
  4. Add some salt and pepper
  5. Add the sauce
